# Public consultation on the draft Minimum Capital Test (MCT) 2026

**Type:** Letter | **No:** MCT | **Category:** Capital Adequacy Requirements | **Date:** 2025-05-22 | **Effective:** 2025-05-22 | **Sector:** Property and Casualty Companies | **Status:** DRAFT OR CONSULTATION | **Tier:** 3

---

- Minimum Capital Test – Guideline (2026)
- Draft P&C Minimum Capital Test and Branch Adequacy of Assets Test PC4 instructions

Today, the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI) is beginning a 90-day consultation on the draft MCT 2026 guideline , along with associated documents. The final MCT 2026 guideline will be published on November 20, 2025, and will come into effect on January 1, 2026.

Key revisions to the MCT 2026 guideline include:

- Simplification of unexpired coverage formula
- Update to regulatory adjustments to net assets available for insurance receivables
- Update to capital confirmation requirements for user fees
- Minor adjustments and clarifications to apply the framework accurately

OSFI will host an Industry Day on June 6, 2025, to provide more details to stakeholders.

Comments can be submitted via email to MCT-TCM@osfi-bsif.gc.ca by end of day August 20, 2025 .

The following is the list of documents included for public consultation:

- Draft of MCT 2026 Guideline
- Draft MCT regulatory reporting form PC4 and instructions
