## MI – Annual/quarterly returns and instructions

No changes were made to the MI Core Financial Quarterly Return (MI3), MI Supervisory Quarterly Return (MI4), MI Supervisory Annual Return (MI5) and the MI Provincial Return (MIPROV) for Q4 2025 or Q1 2026.

Current templates, instructions, and other related documents can be viewed on OSFI's website under Mortgage Insurance Return and Instructions .

## Mortgage Insurers Capital Adequacy Test (MICAT) quarterly return

Changes will be made to the MICAT (MI) return and filing instructions for Q1 2026.

Updated templates, instructions, and other related documents will be posted on our Financial reporting instructions website in due course.

## Updates to assurance attestations – MICAT

OSFI's assurance guideline became effective in November 2022 and requires assurance attestations (attestations) to be filed for all capital, leverage and liquidity regulatory returns, where applicable, as unstructured filings via the Regulatory Reporting System (RRS). The attestations include senior management, opinion of internal auditor, and opinion of external auditor.

Effective Q4 2025, the attestation page of the MICAT return will be updated to include all three attestations. For each attestation, the attestation page and any supporting attachments (for example, signature, auditor's opinion, auditor's report, and summary of unadjusted errors) are to be submitted as one unstructured file in RRS (for example, one PDF or one zip folder).

New submission codes will be available in RRS to file each assurance attestation separately. To identify the correct submission code, refer to the OSFI identification codes on the attestation page. These codes correspond to the "Return title" on the Reporting Requirements for Federally Regulated Mortgage Insurers website.
