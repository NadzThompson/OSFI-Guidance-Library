Audiences: Federally Regulated Mortgage Insurers (FRMIs)

OSFI is issuing a revised version of Guideline B-21 – Residential Mortgage Insurance Underwriting Practices and Procedures . The guideline sets out OSFI’s expectations for prudent residential mortgage insurance underwriting and related activities.

When OSFI published an updated version of Guideline B-20 – Residential Mortgage Underwriting Practices and Procedures , it announced that it would assess Guideline B-21 for consequential amendments. The resulting updates to Guideline B-21 align its language with that of Guideline B-20 in the areas of income verification, property valuation, and fraud detection and prevention.
