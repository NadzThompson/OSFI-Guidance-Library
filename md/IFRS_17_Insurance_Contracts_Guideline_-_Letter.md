- International Financial Reporting Standard (IFRS) 17 - Backgrounder
- IFRS 17 Insurance Contracts Guideline

The Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI) is finalizing its project to support the Canadian insurance industries transition to International Financial Reporting Standard (IFRS) 17 by rescinding the OSFI IFRS 17 Advisory issued to support the transition to the new IFRS insurance contracts accounting standard in Canada and publishing the final IFRS 17 Insurance Contracts (IFRS 17) Guideline. The guideline clarifies the accounting for certain products and transactions and addresses concerns identified by stakeholders during the IFRS 17 transition project.

All federally regulated insurers are subject to the guideline immediately.
