**Type:** Letter | **Category:** Capital Adequacy Requirements | **Date:** 2025-05-22 | **Effective:** 2025-05-22 | **Sector:** Foreign Insurance Branches, Life Insurance and Fraternal Companies | **Status:** FINAL CURRENT | **Tier:** 2

- Regulatory Notice on Adjustments to the Life Insurance Capital Adequacy Test Guideline (2025)

Today, we are publishing a regulatory notice that outlines adjustments to the 2025 Life Insurance Capital Adequacy Test (LICAT) guideline to provide timely and clear guidance to insurers.

The adjustments are related to the capital treatment of certain stop-loss reinsurance arrangements and unregistered reinsurance of segregated fund guarantees and are effective immediately.

OSFI will host an Industry Day on June 6, 2025 to provide more details to stakeholders.
